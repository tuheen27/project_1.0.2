Our Heritage

Founded in 1952 by master watchmaker Henri Chronos, our company has remained family-owned for three generations. We blend traditional Swiss watchmaking techniques with modern innovations to create timepieces of exceptional quality and elegance.

1952

Henri Chronos establishes the workshop in Geneva, creating the first Sovereign timepiece.

1978

Introduction of the Diver series, setting new standards for water-resistant luxury timepieces.

2005

CHRONOS patents its revolutionary self-winding mechanism, used in all our automatic watches today.

Today

With boutiques in 12 countries, CHRONOS continues its legacy of excellence and innovation.
